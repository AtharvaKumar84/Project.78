Privacy and Security

At our BMI Calculator website, we understand the importance of privacy and security when it comes to your personal information. We are committed to ensuring that your data is protected and that you can use our services with confidence. Here's how we prioritize your privacy and security:


Data Confidentiality

We take your privacy seriously. When you use our BMI calculator, rest assured that any information you input remains confidential. We do not store or collect any personally identifiable information unless explicitly provided by you for the purpose of saving your results or creating an account.


Secure Transmission

All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard protocols. This ensures that your information remains private and secure during transmission, reducing the risk of unauthorized access or interception by third parties.


No Third-Party Tracking

We do not engage in any form of third-party tracking or advertising. We believe in respecting your privacy and do not share your data with advertisers or other third parties for marketing purposes. Your use of our BMI calculator is solely for your benefit, and we do not monetize your data in any way.


Anonymous Usage

You have the option to use our BMI calculator anonymously, without creating an account or providing any personal information. This allows you to access our services without worrying about your data being tied to your identity.


Transparent Policies

We are transparent about our data handling practices and privacy policies. Our Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information, as well as your rights and options regarding your data. We encourage you to review our Privacy Policy to understand how we safeguard your privacy.


Regular Security Audits

We regularly conduct security audits and assessments to identify and address any potential vulnerabilities in our systems and infrastructure. By staying vigilant and proactive, we strive to maintain the highest standards of security to protect your data from unauthorized access or breaches.


User Control

You have control over your data. If you choose to save your BMI results or create an account, you can manage your account settings and preferences, including deleting your account and associated data if desired. We believe in empowering you to make informed choices about your privacy and data usage.


At BMI Calculator, your privacy and security are our top priorities. We are committed to providing you with a safe and secure environment to calculate your BMI and access valuable health information.
